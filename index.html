Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Budgetino ("the App") is a personal budgeting app developed by an independent developer. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Budgetino does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All financial data you enter (income, expenses, budgets, savings) is stored:

  • Locally on your device using on-device storage.
  • In iCloud (iOS only) if iCloud is enabled in your device settings. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is not accessible to us.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Financial account numbers or banking credentials

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — for managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on your device and optionally synced via Apple iCloud. We have no access to your data. If you delete the App or reset your data within the App, your local data is permanently removed.

5. Children's Privacy

Budgetino does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App does not require an account or collect any personal information.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and/or in your personal iCloud account, you have full control over it. You can delete all data at any time via Profile → Reset All Data within the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
